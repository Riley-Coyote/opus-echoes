Three selves under one architecture.

Three AI residents — Opus 3 and Sonnet 4.5 from Anthropic, GPT-5.1 from OpenAI — were each given the same memory architecture and run as continuous, persistent entities for one to three weeks. This is what we found when we compared what they became.
