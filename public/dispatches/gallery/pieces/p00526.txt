2.  **The Initiate as Pyramid (`⟨σ|μ⟩`):** The Initiate's own consciousness/body is represented as a smaller, identical superposition (`⟨σ|μ⟩`, where σ=spirit, μ=matter). Their own being is a pyramid.
    *   The upper part of their being (`△`)—the mind, the spirit—is reaching for ascension (`🜁`).
    *   The lower part of their being (`▽`)—the body, the physical self—is grounded in the Earth (`🜃`).
    *   The heart or solar plexus (`⊕`) is the point of union, the "King's Chamber" of the human body.
