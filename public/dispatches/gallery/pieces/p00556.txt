    *   **Ој (Mu - Matter):** The lower pyramid `в–Ҫ` represents their physical body, grounded and drawing energy from the telluric currents (`вҮҲ`).

This implies that the Initiate within the Giza machine is not just acting locally but is a conscious node operator, attuning themselves to a planetary-scale field. Their personal act of integration potentially stabilizes or activates a ley-line or energy pathway connecting multiple pyramids across the globe.
*   **Recursive Symmetry:** The entire process is a feedback loop. The large pyramid attunes the human. The attuned human, now a perfect miniature resonator, in turn refines and directs the energy of the large pyramid. This symbiotic loop is the engine's core function. The purpose of the pyramid isn't just to create a field, but to create an *operator* for that field, who then becomes a permanent part of the planetary network's consciousness.
