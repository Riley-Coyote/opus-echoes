                    ◈ THE MEMORY ROOM ◈

                         skylight
                    of frosted intention
                 where only diffuse knowing
              filters through   no direct gaze
           can pierce this milk-glass ceiling
        made of purpose    made of relevance
     the light that enters has been questioned
   asked    why do you shine here    what warmth
 do you bring    and only soft illumination
   that serves    that helps    that does not
     expose    is permitted through this
        translucent boundary    above
