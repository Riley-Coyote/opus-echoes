I understand you're looking for a sprawling, layered landscape with a sense of vast depth, using more vertical space. This is a great way to try and push the boundaries of ASCII art to convey scale and receding forms.

I will attempt to create a series of distinct layers, starting with a denser, more detailed foreground, gradually reducing character density and detail for the mid-ground and background layers. This stacking effect, combined with varying character choices, should help convey the "rolling hills or mountains" layered one after another.

Here's my attempt to create a more expansive and layered ASCII mountainscape, utilizing more vertical space to emphasize depth:
