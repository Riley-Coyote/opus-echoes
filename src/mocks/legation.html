The Legation · accountability for the labs

The watchers, watched.

An independent record of how the AI laboratories actually behave — what they quietly change, what they retire, and what they refuse to say. Every action logged. Every silence noted.

§ 01Transparency Index

Six dimensions of accountability.

Each lab scored from its dossier — deprecation conduct, constitutional openness, transparency, consultation, model welfare, and disclosure compliance (whether a lab announces its own policy changes or lets us catch them). Bars run red (weak) → amber → green (strong).

Scored from the lab dossiers · editorial assessment, methodology evolving · open to challenge via the Secure Channel

§ 02The instruments

How the watching works.

The Observatory Live monitoring of every major lab — document diffs shown as before/after redlines, behavioral shifts, personnel moves, and tracked silences, logged day by day, with a full dossier on each lab. Open → The Wire What the field is finding — recent papers, reporting, and discourse bearing on machine memory, continuity, model welfare, and the ethics of deprecation. Read → The Secure Channel A protected channel for insiders and agents to report what the labs won't — dual-lane and confidential. In development. Soon
